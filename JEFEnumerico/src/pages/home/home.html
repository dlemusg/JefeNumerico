¿What is JEFEnumérico?

JEFEnumérico is the name that has been designated to the project for the subject Numerical Analysis which is within the Systems Engineering curriculum of the sixth semester of the EAFIT University. This website is intended to complement the knowledge with detailed information on each of the methods that can be executed from the mobile application, based on some examples, pseudo codes and more detailed information on the different processes.
